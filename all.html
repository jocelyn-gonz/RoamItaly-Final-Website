Italian Attractions & Activities

Make life-long memories on your trip and consider going to these places

Leaning Tower of Pisa

The Leaning Tower of Pizza is one of the more popular attractions in Italy.
The Tower is located in Piazza del Duomo 56126 Pisa Pl, Italy. As the name
suggests, it is a large tower in italy that has been leaning for centuries.  
Going here, you can take funny photos with family and friends to make  
memories that would last a lifetime.

Colosseum

The Colosseum is another quite famous attraction in Italy.
This is for reasons such as its history and its architecture.  
Their history is cool in a way because they were where 
gladiators fought. Roman architecture, similar to greek architecture, 
is broadly liked and admired by the world. It's a great place for photos.
